Method

How Calibre reads fit

The model compares player traits with team demands. Perfect alignment scores high. A mismatch does not mean the player is bad; it means the tactical environment may not unlock him.

1. Traits

Each player carries six simple tactical traits: press, tempo, chance, carry, defend and role.

2. Demands

Each team has six system demands based on how that team wants to play.

3. Verdict

The fit score translates into a football-language verdict that is easier to use than raw numbers.
